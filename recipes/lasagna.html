Lasagna

Classic lasagna recipe

This classic lasagna recipe has an easy meat sauce as the base, layered with noodles and cheese.
This is great for feeding a big family, and will freeze well.
The key for this recipe is the addition of a little sugar to the sauce and using a bit more cheese than other recipes.

Ingredients:

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef chuck
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced (about 3/4 cup)
  • 1/2 large bell pepper, diced (~ 3/4 cup)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can good-quality tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano
  • 1 tablespoon to 1/4 cup sugar (to taste, optional)
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 dry lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 1/2 pounds mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1/4 pound freshly grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Put pasta water on to boil
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Cook the bell pepper, onions, and garlic; add back the beef
  4. Make the sauce; add beef mixture, tomatoes (crushed, sauce, paste)
    sprinkle sugar, salt to taste
  5. Bring sauce to simmer, lower heat to maintain lower simmer for 15-45 minutes, stirring often.
  6. Remove from heat
  7. Boil and drain lasagna noodles
  8. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
  9. Assemble lasagna layers (meat sauce, pasta, cheese)
  10. Bake for 45 minutes
  11. Cool and serve!
