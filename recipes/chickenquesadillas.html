Easy Chicken Quesadillas

Incredible Chicken Quesadillas packed with juicy chicken, peppers, onions, garlic, and lots of melty cheese.
They are extremely easy to make and a healthy idea that is perfect for nights where you are in a rush.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breasts
  • Oil
  • Diced bell pepper
  • Diced onion
  • Jalapeno
  • Garlic
  • Taco seasoning
  • Shredded cheese
  • Medium flour tortillas

Steps:

  1. Chop chicken into bite-sized pieces
  2. Warm oil and add chicken with taco seasoning
  3. Saute chicken for 5 minutes then add onions, peppers, and garlic
    saute for 5 more minutes
  4. Remove ingredients from pan
  5. Add 1/2 teaspoon of oil on pan
  6. Add flour tortilla with cheese, chicken, and vegetable mixture
  7. Add another tortilla on top
  8. Cook until both tortillas are crispy and golden brown
  9. Enjoy!
