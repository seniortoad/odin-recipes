Easy Chicken Quesadillas

Incredible Chicken Quesadillas packed with juicy chicken, peppers, onions, garlic, and lots of melty cheese.
They are extremely easy to make and a healthy idea that is perfect for nights where you are in a rush.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breasts
  • Oil
  • Diced bell pepper
  • Diced onion
  • Jalapeno
  • Garlic
  • Taco seasoning
  • Shredded cheese
  • Medium flour tortillas
