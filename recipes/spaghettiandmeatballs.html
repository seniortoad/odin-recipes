Spaghetti and Meatballs

Classic Spaghetti and Meatballs

A take on the famous spaghetti and meatballs recipe known world wide.
This dish is savory, juicy, and combines grains and meat in the most perfect way.
Perfect for a casual family dinner night, or to impress anyone with your cooking skills!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • salt
  • 1 1/4 pounds ground sirloin
  • 2 teaspoons worcestershire sauce
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup beef stock
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • handful of chopped parsley
  • 10 leaves of fresh basil, thinly sliced
